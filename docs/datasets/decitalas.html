Desītālas¶

Śārngadeva (शार्ङ्गदेव) compiled a list of 130 rhythmic fragments called desītālas (देसी ताल) in his 13th-century musicological treatise, the Sangītaratnākara (सङ्गीतरत्नाकर). “Desi” refers to the provinces of India in which the fragments were collected. They are encoded in the fragments_db.db file in the databases directory. They are also provided as XML files in the fragments directory.
